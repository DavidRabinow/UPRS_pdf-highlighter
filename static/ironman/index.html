The Iron Man helmet, inspired by the iconic movie prop, was meticulously 3D-printed to replicate its sleek, high-tech design. Inside, two servo motors are mounted at the top, allowing the faceplate to rise and fall smoothly with a button press on the side of the helmet. Creating this functionality required custom coding to ensure precise control and synchronization of the servo motors.
